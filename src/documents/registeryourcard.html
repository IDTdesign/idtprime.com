REGISTER YOUR CARD

Welcome to the registration page. You’re only a few clicks away from additional security, and even more spending options.

Registering your Prime Card is optional, but it does provide you with a number of extra benefits.

By registering your card, you will be free to – make purchases online, over the phone, and by mail order. This is because these transactions often require you to submit a name and address.

Registering your card also gives you protection in the event that your Prime Card is lost or stolen. As long as your card is registered with us, we will happily provide you with a replacement card for a small fee.

So take a few moments to register, and enjoy shopping with even more peace of mind.
